About Reaction Test

Reaction Test is a mobile app with two reflex-based games to challenge your speed and reaction times:

  • Lights Out: Press the button as soon as the traffic light turns off.
  • Light Buttons: Press as many buttons as possible quickly to score points and climb the leaderboard!

Global Leaderboard

Compete with players worldwide! Your scores in Light Buttons are saved to a global leaderboard. The faster you are, the higher you'll rank!
